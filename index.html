Privacy Policy for the Geo Silencer App
The Location Silencer App is designed to help you avoid disturbing others in important places.
Here's what you need to know about the privacy of your information when using the app:
Information Collection: No information is collected, any and all information given by the user is
stored on their device storage system.
Use of Information: Location information is used to determine your location and silence your
phone in designated places. I will never sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for
any purposes.
Security: I take security seriously and do my best to protect your information. However, no
online method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. So, I can't guarantee absolute
security of your information.
Policy Changes: I may update this Privacy Policy if I make any changes to the app or the way I
handle your information. If that happens, I'll post the updated policy on this page.
If you have any questions or concerns about the app or your privacy, feel free to reach out to me
at briansong2002@gmail.com.
Thanks for using the Geo Silencer App! I hope it helps you avoid disturbing others in important
places.
